### Peer Review Checklist

When reviewing, please address:
1. Literature & Citations
   - Are primary references included and correctly cited?
   - Are there missing important works?

2. Logical coherence
   - Do the postulational steps follow logically?
   - Are any hidden assumptions present?

3. Formalization & Math
   - Are inequalities correctly used?
   - Are numerical claims justified or flagged as estimates?

4. Compatibility with experiment
   - Does the claim contradict known empirical constraints?

5. Falsifiability
   - Are suggested tests plausible? Are clear falsification criteria proposed?

6. Safety / Ethics
   - Does the contribution contain instructions or suggestions that could endanger people? If yes, flag.

Please add inline suggestions or open issues for substantial items.
